# Material Customization Quick Guide

## How to Use Material Customization

### Step-by-Step

1. **Select Furniture**
   - Click on any furniture item in the 3D scene
   - Item will show green selection ring

2. **Open Material Picker**
   - Click the **🎨 Material** button in the top toolbar
   - Material picker modal will appear

3. **Browse Materials**
   - Use category tabs to filter:
     - **All** - Show all 14 materials
     - **🌱 Organic** - Natural materials (wood, bamboo, mycelium)
     - **⚡ Tech** - High-tech materials (solar, metal, bio-plastic)
     - **🔀 Hybrid** - Mixed materials (living vine, tech wood)
     - **✨ Luminous** - Glowing materials (bioluminescent, crystal)

4. **Preview & Select**
   - Each material shows:
     - Color preview swatch
     - Emoji icon + name
     - Category badge
     - Description
   - Click any material to apply it instantly

5. **Save Your Design**
   - Material selection persists in saved layouts
   - Use the **Save** button in Layout Controls panel

## Available Materials

- **🌳 Eco Wood** - Reclaimed sustainable wood
- **🎋 Living Bamboo** - Fast-growing bamboo
- **🪨 Coral Stone** - Lab-grown coral-like composite
- **🍄 Mycelium Foam** - Biodegradable fungal material

### ⚡ Tech (High-Tech & Modern)

- **⚡ Solar Panel** - Photovoltaic surface
- **♻️ Recycled Metal** - Upcycled metal alloy
- **🧪 Bio-Plastic** - Plant-derived polymer
- **🔮 Smart Glass** - Adaptive glass (semi-transparent)

### 🔀 Hybrid (Bio-Tech Fusion)

- **🏗️ Green Concrete** - Carbon-negative concrete
- **🌿 Living Vine** - Self-growing plant-fiber hybrid
- **🪵 Tech Wood** - Conductive polymer-infused wood

### ✨ Luminous (Glowing & Bioluminescent)

- **💡 Bioluminescent** - Glowing organisms (bright green)
- **💎 Crystal Light** - Photon-storing crystal (blue glow)
- **🌟 Neon Moss** - Fluorescent moss (neon green)

## Tips

- **Experiment Freely**: Changes are instant and reversible with Ctrl+Z
- **Mix & Match**: Different furniture can have different materials
- **Save Often**: Materials persist in saved layouts
- **Theme Consistency**: Use materials from the same category for cohesive designs
- **Contrast**: Mix organic and tech materials for interesting contrasts

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Click furniture** → Select
- **🎨 Material button** → Open picker
- **Esc** → Close picker / Deselect furniture
- **Ctrl+Z** → Undo material change
- **Ctrl+Y** → Redo material change
